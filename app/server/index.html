Welcome to saffron!

Create your own visualizer by logging in and pressing the add button. Get started on your visualizer with p5.js. Need some inspiration? The Chrome Music Lab showcases some pretty great visualizers, or you can look at our very own participants' submissions below!

saffron is a collection of visualizers made by participants of Music Hacks 2017. This event is hosted by Freetail Hackers at the University of Texas at Austin.
